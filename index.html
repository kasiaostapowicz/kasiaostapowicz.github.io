About me

Working at the intersection of environmental & data science

I am a research scientist in Environmental Data Science and Earth Observation, working at the intersection of Land System Science, biodiversity, and climate research. My work focuses on land-use and land-cover change, ecosystem dynamics, and human–environment interactions across Arctic, boreal, and temperate regions.

I develop spatial modelling frameworks that integrate satellite time series, spatial analysis, and GeoAI to investigate long-term environmental change and climate-driven ecosystem responses.

In addition to my research, I have experience leading and managing research laboratories as well as international and national research projects, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and knowledge exchange. I am also dedicated to teaching and mentoring students at various academic levels.
